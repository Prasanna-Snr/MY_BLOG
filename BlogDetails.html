About

Hey there! I'm Prasanna Sunuwar, a passionate traveler eager to explore the wonders of this picturesque city nestled in the Himalayas. Through this series, I'll be sharing my experiences, insights, and tips as I dive into Pokhara's rich culture, breathtaking landscapes, and thrilling adventures. Join me on this immersive exploration, and let's uncover the magic of Pokhara together!
